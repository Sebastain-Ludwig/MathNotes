## 导数的四则运算

设$u(x),v(x)$均可导,则:

* $(u(x)\pm v(x))^{'}=u(x)^{'}\pm v(x)^{'}$或者$(v\pm v)^{'}=u^{'}\pm v^{'}$
* $(uv)^{'}=u^{'}v+uv^{'}$或者$(Cu)^{'}=Cu^{'}$
* $(\frac{u}{v})^{'}=\frac{u^{'}v-uv^{''}}{v^{2}},(v\neq 0)$或者$(\frac{1}{v})^{'}=\frac{v^{'}}{v^{2}}$
